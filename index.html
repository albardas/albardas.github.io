Everyone has the right to freedom of movement and residence within the borders of each State, also to leave any country, including his own and to return to his country

North Corea has banned freedom of movementclose

It is illegal for the North Korean people to leave their country without the government permission.

Regime attempts to restrict the people’s movement even inside their own country.

If you wish to travel to another part of the country, you are supposed to have a specific purpose and obtain permission from your work unit.

If you do not live in the capital where more resources are concentrated, you will likely be denied access.

In my opinion a good solution to this issue is to create another organization that could force these countries to provide every universal human right.

Certainly there are organizations that promote justice and dignity for everyone, they stand up for human rights, encourage other organizations to help and present real information about these issues in order to create change.

If there was an organization that could hold more power then all of these countries would had to follow the rules about human and civil rights.

Charitable Organizationmore_vert
Human Rights Watch close

The Human Rights Watch is a nonprofit, nongovernmental human rights organization made up of roughly 400 staff members around the globe. Its staff consists of human rights professionals including country experts, lawyers, journalists, and academics of diverse backgrounds and nationalities. They are dedicated to protecting the human rights of people around the world.

The Human Rights Watch meets with governments, the United Nations, regional groups like the African Union and the European Union, financial institutions, and corporations to press for changes in policy and practices that promote human rights and justice around the world.
